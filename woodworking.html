Woodworking

Hardwood furniture and objects — designed and built by hand

Shaker Table

A classic shaker side table built from hard maple with walnut accent strips in the top. Features a small dovetailed drawer and tapered legs. Built following traditional shaker proportions with hand-cut joinery.

Wall Mounted Table

A self-designed wall-mounted folding table with a walnut top and white oak base. The angular bracket geometry was designed from scratch to be both structural and sculptural. Folds flat against the wall when not in use.

Dice Rolling Box

A self-designed hexagonal dice rolling box built from cherry and maple with walnut inlay chevrons on the lid. Hinged with a brass hinge and sized for tabletop gaming.
