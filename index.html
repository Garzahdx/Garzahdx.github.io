About

Tom Reynolds is a builder, tinkerer, and systems thinker who enjoys solving practical problems. With experience spanning carpentry, electrical systems, mechanical work, and installation, he approaches projects with a focus on functionality and durability.

When he's not building or troubleshooting something, Tom can usually be found hiking long trails, traveling in a camper van, or exploring new ways to simplify and improve everyday systems.
